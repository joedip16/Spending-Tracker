50:30:20 Budget Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

50:30:20 Budget Tracker helps users track income, expenses, budget categories, goals, recurring transactions, and spending insights. This policy explains what information the app stores and how users can control it.

Information We Collect

The app may store the following information when you use account sync:

  • Email address used to create or sign in to your Firebase account.
  • Budget profile settings, such as name, single or joint setup, and household label.
  • Transactions you add or import, including dates, descriptions, amounts, categories, and purchase type.
  • Budget goals, category settings, recurring transaction rules, skipped recurring occurrences, theme preference, and dashboard preferences.

How Information Is Stored

When sync is enabled, app data is stored in Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Firebase is provided by Google. The app also stores a local copy in your browser using localStorage so it can load quickly on your device.

How Information Is Used

Your information is used to provide the app's core features: signing in, syncing across devices, showing budget summaries, calculating charts and insights, importing transactions, exporting backups, and restoring your settings.

Sharing

The app does not sell your personal information. Data is processed by Firebase/Google as the cloud service provider needed to run authentication and sync. If you export a backup file, you control where that exported file is stored or shared.

Security

Firestore rules restrict synced budget data so signed-in users can only access their own budget tracker record. No security system is perfect, so users should use a strong password and keep their devices secure.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and synced budget data from Settings under Account Sync. This deletes your Firestore budget data, deletes your Firebase Authentication account, and clears the local app data stored in that browser.

Data Retention

Your synced data remains stored while your account exists. If you delete your account/data, the app attempts to permanently remove your synced budget record and account immediately.

Children

This app is not intended for children under 13. Do not use the app if you are not old enough to manage your own financial information under the laws that apply to you.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion help, contact the app developer at the support email listed wherever the app is distributed.
